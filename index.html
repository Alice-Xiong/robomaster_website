About

We are a multidisciplinary competitive robotics team at UBC, Vancouver.

We compete annually in RoboMaster, one of the world's most technically and strategically intensive robotics competitions hosted by DJI in Shenzhen, China.

Through competitive robotics, our members develop expertise and enthusiasm in robotics, design, embedded systems, and vision algorithms.

How can I join UBC RoboMaster?


Our winter recruitment cycle has now closed. Check back in early September for fall recruitment information. As well, follow us on Facebook for updates and outreach events.

What time commitment is expected?

The team holds weekly meetings on Sunday and subteam meetings one evening of the week. A minimum time commitment of 10 hours is expected from each member. As competition deadlines approach, the workload will naturally ramp up and additional commitment will be announced.

Where can I find more about the competition?

The official RoboMaster website contains many useful information on how the competition works. You can also checkout their official Twitch channel for exciting match footages.

Who are you looking for?


This team is open to all UBC students. We are looking for enthusiasm in robotics as well as time commitment towards learning and solving difficult problems. Our four main streams include mechanical design, embedded systems, vision algorithms, and outreach projects.
